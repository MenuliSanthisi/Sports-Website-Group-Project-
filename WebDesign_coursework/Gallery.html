International Sport Events







FIFA



International Asscociation Football Competition contested by senior memeers of FIFA. Tournament is held every four year. 32 teams compete for the title at venues within host Nations over a month.

Olympics



Summer Olympics Game is a major multi-sport event normally held once every 4 years. The inausural took place in 1896 Athens, Greece.

ICC World Cup



ICC Men's Cricket WOrld Cup is the International Championship of ODI Cricket. Organiszed by the ICC, every 4 year. The tournament is one of the most viewed sporting events.

NFL League



National Football League-NFL is a professional American foorball league that consists of 32 teams, divided equally between AFC and NFC. The leagueis headquartered in New York City.

UEFA League



UEFA league ia an annual club football competetion organized by the Union of European Football Asscociation by top-division European Clubs.

Wimbledon



Wimbledon Championship is the oldest tennis tournament in the world and is widely regarded as the most prestigious. It has been held at the All England Club in Wimbledon, London.

RWC



Rugby World Cup is Men's rugby union tournament contested every 4 years between top International teams. The winners are awarded the Webb Ellis Cup, named after who invented rugby.

Tour de France



The Tour de France is an annual men's multiple-stage bicycle race primialy held in France, while also occasionally passing through nearby countries. It consist of 21 stages, each a day long, over the course of 23 days, conciding with Bastille Day Holiday.

NBA



National Basketball Assosiation is a professional basketball league in Northern American composed of 30 teams. It is one of the major professional sports league in US and Canada and is considered the premier men's professional basketball league in the world.

IAAF



World Athletics, formally known as International Association Athletic Federation is an International governing body for sports of athletics, covering the track and field, cross country running, road running, mountain running and ultra running.

IIHF



The Ice Hockey World Championship are annual International men's Ice Hockey Federation. First it was offically held at the 1920 Summer Olympics, it is the sport's highest profile Annual International Tournament.

Commonwealth



The Commonwealth Games, often referred to as the Friendly Games. The Games are overseen by the Commonwealth Games Federation(CGF), which controls the sporting programme and selects host cities. The games movement consists of international sports federations (IFs), Commonwealth Games Associations (CGAs) and organising committees for each specific Commonwealth Games.
